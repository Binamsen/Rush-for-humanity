About Us

Rush For Humanity is a NGO working for childrens.It was established on '17 Oct 2015'. On '25th April 2015',Nepal was hit by a massive earthquake. Due to the earthquake many childrens in Nepal were in need of help. Rush For Humanity started its development initiatives in nepal by providing foods,shelter,clothes,books and other resources to the childrens affected by earthquake.Rush For Humanity formally started its long-term development work in Nepal after signing both general and project agreement with the Social Welfare Council in 2015.


This organization has visited to all the affected areas of Nepal and monitored the condition of childrens at those areas and provided resources to those childrens.Rush For Humanity has funded resources in districts namely Kathmandu,Bhaktapur,Sindhupalchowk, Gorkha,Lalitpur,Kirtipur,Makwanpur.


At first, this organization was mainly focused on those areas affected by earthquake.But now it has also been involved in developing the education system and providing necessary resources to the childrens of rural areas of Nepal.

Programme Partnership

Rush For Humanity believes that partnering with others is a key factor in bringing about lasting changes in communities. RFH partners with government, community groups, NGOs and the private sector. Rush For Humanity adopts a multi-stakeholder approach to partnering, recognising that all of these different groups have an important role in creating change. The nature of the partnering relationship varies depending on the issues being addressed, and the resources and capacity of the different partners involved.

How we are accountable and funded

Rush For Humanity is accountable to private and government donors, to public agencies charged with legal oversight, to our funding offices and to those whom we serve. Internal and external audits and evaluations are conducted regularly to ensure efficient and effective use of resources.


We receive funding from private donors and bilateral agencies through our support partners in RFH Australia, RFH Canada, RFH Hong Kong, RFH Korea, RFH Switzerland, RFH Taiwan, RFH Japan, RFH Singapore and RFH UK.
