Nepal


Rush For Humanity continues to support childrens affected by earthquake.

Nepal is among the poorest and least-developed countries in the world. Half the population of Nepal comprises of children below 18 years. Children are more vulnerable, particularly when combined with poverty, as they can fall prey to abuse, exploitation and neglect.


On 25thApril 2015, Nepal was hit by a massive earthquake of 7.6. Due to this earthquake large number of childrens were affected. Due to earthquake, there was huge loss of food,shelter and other necessary resources. Exposure to infectious diseases, malnutrition, and poor hygiene and sanitation and lack of a healthy environment affects the health of children. Childrens were living in fear. They were not getting education as well. Along with the earthquake the poverty range in nepal also increased more. Many childrens are under the poverty range.


Rush For Humanity is working to address the immediate poverty issues confronting children and vulnerable communities in Nepal integrating its work efforts in advocacy, development and humanitarian emergency affairs.


Rush For Humanity has been providing the childrens with all the necessary resources such as food,shelter,education,clothes,health.
